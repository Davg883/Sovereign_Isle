The Two-Step Master Operator Flow

From Base Plate to investor-grade narrative — a disciplined, human-in-the-loop pipeline.

Modern AI can generate beautiful images. But strategy requires repeatable assets that withstand scrutiny. Here’s the pipeline that turns a clean, character-locked portrait into a cinematic, investor-grade narrative frame — without sacrificing truth or taste.

Step 1 — Base Plate (Character-Locked Portrait)

We begin with a grounded, cinematic portrait — the strategist at the helm. No overlays. No visual noise. This “clean plate” locks face geometry, wardrobe and light direction. It prevents character drift and keeps the story anchored in reality.

Acceptance Gate A: Face geometry within CCK tolerance • Wardrobe exact • Light direction matches LUT • Export master PNG (4K+) with alpha matte.

The Two-Step Flow at a Glance

Step 2 — Human-Directed Augmentation

In post (Photoshop/AE), we introduce meaning, not noise. Journal words drive gold filaments that wrap (with correct occlusion) across rigging and silhouette. Grade last; ensure LUT parity and unified grain so the frame sits cleanly inside the brand’s system.

  • Metaphor correctness: Emission from words only.
  • Physics of light: Contact glow on nearby surfaces (~3–8 cm).
  • Silhouette readability: Eyes and mouth remain unobscured.
Acceptance Gate B: At 1200 px width, journal text is legible and real.

The Investor-Grade Frame

The result is a cinematic, truthful image that carries narrative weight in decks, homepages and PR — consistent across campaigns because the Base Plate is locked and augmentation is governed by explicit gates. It’s how we turn “pretty” into “performant”.

Implementation Notes (for teams)

  • Exports: Keep a 4K+ master PNG for the Base Plate and a layered comp for augmentation.
  • Parity: Grade last; verify against your Hero LUT; apply unified grain.
  • Reuse: New campaigns swap journal source and filament paths; character remains consistent.
